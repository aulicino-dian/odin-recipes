Ginger Steak

Description

This is a simple recipe and it is one of my personal favorites. After some experimentation, it has become a perfect dish!

Ingredients

  • 4 8 ounce beef sirloin steaks, at least 3/4 inches thick
  • 2 tablespoons of soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon of ground ginger
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1 tablespoon perpared yellow mustard
  • 1 teaspoon lemon juice

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven's broiler.
  2. In a small bowl, mix together the soy sauce, ginger salt, pepper, basil, mustard and lemon juice until smooth, place the steaks on a broiling pan and pour 1/4 of the mixture over each on. Massage into the meat
  3. Broil the steaks for 5 minutes, then turn over and cook to you rdesired degree of donenes
